About MCA's Pizza Palace

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by Mario Rossi, our family-owned pizzeria has been serving authentic Italian pizzas for over 35 years. What started as a small neighborhood restaurant has grown into a beloved local institution.

We use only the finest ingredients, from our hand-tossed dough made fresh daily to our signature sauce crafted from San Marzano tomatoes. Every pizza is made with passion and attention to detail.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to destination for pizza lovers in the community.

Mario Rossi

Founder & Head Chef
